Content Evaluation Process

Choose a YouTube content category based on skill and technical requirements

We evaluated categories like tutorials, day-in-a-life and interview videos. The vlogger category seemed the best fit since vloggers can both inform and create a connection with their viewers.

Watch content from the chosen categories

  • We reviewed popular vlogs. Their ability to connect with their viewers through casual language was effective in convincing the audience to take action.

  • It is important to differentiate between vloggers who focus purely on entertainment and those who promote causes or raise awareness. For this fundraising concert, we need vloggers who include meaningful causes into their regular content.

    • Find a need within the chosen YouTube category

  • The vlogger will need to connect our concert's cause into their content in a tone that’s natural, light and relatable. This way the viewers won’t feel forced to help the cause.


  • There’s a need for more vlogs that are both entertaining and with a purpose.
