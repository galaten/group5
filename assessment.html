Content Strategy

Explaining our group's strategy and our channel plan

Our strategy

  • Share exclusive behind-the-scenes content, such as how the team are busy with pre-concert preparations or how the bands who will play onstage are feeling about participating.

  • The vlogger can share a story or anecdote to explain why the cause matters personally to them.

  • Host a live stream where fans can ask questions about the cause, the concert, or the vlogger's life, and be encouraged to buy tickets and donate in real-time.

  • In the vlogger's videos, give personal shoutouts to donors and concert ticket buyers, creating a sense of recognition and inspiring more viewers to do the same.

    • Call to Action

  • Every video will end with a clear, engaging call to action that encourages fans to donate, attend the concert, or both.
