Plan Overview

Based on the evaluation using the instructions and guidelines, our group has identified our chosen categories that we will focus on:
Chosen Category: Vlogger
Target Audience: Teens and young adults (13-24 years old)

Needs

  • The audience needs someone who can inspire them to take action. Influential vlogger can provide the connection and engagement this audience needs.
  • Audiences in this age group are looking for authentic influencers who speak honestly and in a way they can relate to.
  • To promote a concert for a cause, the information needs to be both informative and entertaining. A skilled vlogger can explain the cause clearly and make it feel like a personal conversation.
  • The content must inspire viewers to take action: attending the concert, joining as a volunteer or donating to the fund.
